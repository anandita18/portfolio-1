ABOUT ME.

I’m a Master's student at the University of Michigan School of Information specializing in Human Computer Interaction and UX Design. I graduated from the University of Washington, Seattle with a degree in Psychology and a minor in Informatics.


I aim to design engaging products that create emotional connections and leave a lasting and positive impact. I love to design in dynamic environments that encourage me to not only practice design, but learn and adapt new design skills and principles. When I’m not designing digital products, you can find me reading cookbooks, drinking coffee, or eating the free samples at Costco.
